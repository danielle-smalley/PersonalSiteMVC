About Me

Hi! My name is Danielle Smalley and I am a full stack web developer.

I am skilled in front-end, middle-tier, and back end development. This includes JavaScript, C#/.Net, jQuery, SQL, JSON, ReactJS, and more.

Over the past 9 years, I’ve become a successful advocate, project manager, and problem anticipator/resolver for clients in the roles I’ve held.

I have a knack for empathizing and listening to clients’ needs, communicating transparently and directly, and tirelessly working towards solutions.

My interest in technology started when I was a little girl, enjoying computers, puzzles, and video games. Now, I see a need for more women in tech and am driven to contribute to this mission. This led me to enroll in Centriq’s full stack web development program. I dove right in and haven’t looked back!

I’m looking forward to bringing my unique background, my love of helping others, my passion for learning and facing new challenges, and my eagerness to continuously grow to my tech career.
